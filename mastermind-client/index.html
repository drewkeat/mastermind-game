How to Play:

Goal:
Guess the secret combination hidden under the boxes at the top of the board.

Can you guess the right colors in the right order?

Directions:
1) Drag colored pegs into one of the empty holes on the "guess row". Then click the "check guess" button.
2) Use the feedback to help you deduce the correct combination.

Feedback Pegs:

BLACK:

One of your pegs is the correct color, but not in the right place.

RED:

One of your pegs is the right color, and in the right place!

Try to get 4 RED PEGS!
